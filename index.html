Glam story from Los Angeles

Who better than a global beauty icon to embody a 100% glamorous makeup brand? So here is Kylie Jenner's ambition: to make glamor simple and accessible to all thanks to quality products, "suitable for vegans" and a wide variety of shades so as not to neglect any skin tone. Long-wearing, hyper-pigmented formulas, innovative textures… We can say it: the young woman has transmitted to her products the right blend of daring and elegant simplicity. These are the products that she herself uses on a daily basis; products that adapt to the state of mind, allowing a natural look one day and pleasant the next. In short: an ode to freedom!

Lipsticks products

  • The iconic Matte Lip Kit: this Kit consists of a matte liquid lipstick and a lip liner, in short, the ideal duo for matte and full lips. Available in 21 shades, that leaves your mouth speechless!

  • The Matte Liquid Lipstick: a liquid lipstick with a creamy formula, which does not dry out the lips and guarantees a mat and flawless finish for 8 hours. Available in 8 shades, all pigmented at will!
